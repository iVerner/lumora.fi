## Release and Permission

For images approved under this release, the Model authorizes the Photographer to edit, change, crop, retouch, copy, reproduce, display, publish, distribute, license for publication, and otherwise use those images for lawful professional, artistic, portfolio, promotional, editorial, exhibition, competition, print, press, web, social media, magazine, journal, book, article, presentation, and similar publication uses.

This permission includes use by the Photographer and publication or display by magazines, journals, books, galleries, exhibitions, portfolio reviewers, websites, social media platforms, printers, editors, agents, and similar professional or artistic publication partners involved in the approved use of the images.

The Photographer may decide how the approved images are edited, sequenced, cropped, displayed, captioned, and presented, provided the use remains within this release and any written privacy limits.

The Photographer owns the copyright to the photographs under applicable copyright law. This release does not transfer copyright to the Model and does not give the Model ownership of the image files.

## Usage Limits

Approved images may not be used for adult entertainment advertising, escort-style promotion, paid content platforms, defamatory use, misleading endorsement, political endorsement, or unrelated commercial product advertising without separate written agreement.

Approved images may be used only for lawful purposes and may not be presented in a way that falsely suggests the Model endorses an unrelated product, service, political cause, or organization.

## Rights and Claims

For approved uses within the written scope of this release, the Model releases the Photographer from claims connected to editing, change, cropping, retouching, copying, publication, display, distribution, licensing for publication, or promotional use of the approved images.

This release covers only the approved uses listed above. It does not apply to uses outside the approved scope, unlawful use, false endorsement, or uses that breach the privacy limits written in this document.

## Withdrawal or Changes

If the Model later has concerns about a published approved image, they should contact the Photographer. Removal or changes will be considered in good faith, but a concern or removal request does not automatically cancel this release or the approved scope.

This does not require the Photographer to recall printed materials, remove third-party publications, recover cached copies, undo prepared publications, stop uses already made within the approved scope, or stop future approved uses unless the Photographer agrees to that change in writing.

This does not affect any rights the Model may have under applicable data protection law. Those rights will be considered together with the Photographer's legitimate interests, legal rights, authorship interests, archive needs, freedom-of-expression interests, and already prepared, published, distributed, licensed, printed, cached, or third-party uses.
