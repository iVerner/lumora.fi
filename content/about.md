Lumora is the boudoir and fine art nude photography work of Ignat Kudriavtsev, based in Helsinki, Finland.

The project focuses on intimate portraiture, natural light, shadow, privacy, and quiet collaboration. The work is personal and artistic rather than commercial: there are no paid sessions, no packages, and no booking flow. Current collaborations are TFP only.

My approach is calm and careful. I am interested in images that feel honest, restrained, and respectful: body language, texture, negative space, fabric, and the atmosphere of a private moment.

Trust matters more than performance. Before a shoot, we talk about boundaries, comfort, anonymity, publication, and the kind of images that would feel meaningful to make. Nothing is assumed, and nothing is published without review and written approval.

Communication is usually in English. Finnish or Russian may also be possible depending on the context.
